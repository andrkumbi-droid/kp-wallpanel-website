About Us

A Family Business
You Can Trust

KP Wallpanel is a family-run business based in Pathum Thani, Thailand. For over 2 years we have been supplying high-quality WPC wall panels and PVC ceiling panels to homeowners, hotels, resorts, and retail partners across Thailand.

We offer a wide range of designs at competitive prices. For large orders or wholesale inquiries, we welcome custom requests. Every order includes free clip-locks, and we offer free delivery nationwide.
