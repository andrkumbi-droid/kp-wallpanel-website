A Family Business

KP Wallpanel is a family-run business based in Pathum Thani, Thailand. For over 2 years we have been supplying high-quality WPC wall panels and PVC ceiling panels to homeowners, hotels, resorts, and retail partners across Thailand.

We offer competitive prices, welcome large orders and wholesale partnerships. Every order includes free clip-locks and free delivery nationwide.
